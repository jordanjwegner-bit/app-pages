Privacy Policy — Splice

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Splice is built to be private by default.

What we collect

Nothing. Splice does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information or usage data.

  • No accounts. There is nothing to sign up for.
  • No analytics or tracking. The app contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • No network access. The daily puzzle is computed from the date on your device and works fully offline.
  • Local save data only. Your in-progress reel, streaks, statistics and settings are stored on your device (and in your personal device backups, which we never see). Deleting the app deletes the save.

Purchases and ratings

If a future update adds optional purchases or advertising, this policy and the App Store privacy label will be updated first, before any such feature ships. Rating prompts use Apple's built-in review system; we never see what you tap.

Children

Splice is a general-audience game (rated 9+) and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes

Material changes to this policy will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
