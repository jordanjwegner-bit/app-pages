Privacy Policy — Pocket Arcade Club Poster

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Pocket Arcade Club Poster is an internal publishing tool operated by Pocket Arcade Club. It posts short gameplay trailers of our own iPhone games to our own TikTok account. It is not offered to the public and has no users other than us.

What we collect

Nothing from anyone else. The tool handles only:

  • Our own videos — gameplay clips we produce ourselves.
  • Our own TikTok account credentials — an access token stored privately on our own hardware, used solely to publish to our own account via TikTok's official API.

What we don't do

  • No collection of personal information from any third party.
  • No tracking, analytics, cookies, or advertising.
  • No selling or sharing of data with anyone.
  • No access to any TikTok account other than our own.

Data retention

Videos and captions exist on our own machines and, once published, on our TikTok profile. Delete requests are moot: there is no third-party data to delete.
